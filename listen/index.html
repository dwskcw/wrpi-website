How To Listen

The easiest way to listen if you're within our broadcast range (see below) is to tune in on 91.5 FM! If you're outside our range or would otherwise rather stream, search for WRPI on your favorite radio streaming app.

If you can't find us on a radio app, download VLC from their website or your device's app store, and download the webstream file here. Run VLC and play the downloaded file there. This file streams directly from our webstream to your device. Apologies for any security warnings, we're working on it!
